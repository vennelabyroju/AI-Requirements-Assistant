Attendees:

Discussion:

The warehouse team requires real-time inventory visibility.

Low stock alerts should be generated automatically when inventory falls below predefined thresholds.

Only supervisors and managers should be authorised to approve stock adjustments.

Reports should be generated weekly and emailed to operations management.

Barcode scanning should update inventory immediately.
